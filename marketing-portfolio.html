Apex Build Co.

What I built

An end-to-end automated lead generation system for a construction company. The goal was to capture leads from Facebook Ads, move them into a CRM, trigger email sequences, and book consultations automatically.

How it works

  • Facebook Ads captured leads through a form targeting potential clients
  • Zapier connected the ad form to HubSpot CRM, creating contact records automatically
  • New contacts were synced to a segmented Mailchimp audience and triggered a consultation email sequence
  • Calendly links in the emails let leads book a consultation directly
  • A Google Sheets pipeline tracked lead status and progress through the funnel
  • Content was scheduled through Meta Business Suite and tasks managed in ClickUp

What I did

Wrote and automated a personalised cold email campaign introducing AutoWave Pro's AI capabilities to potential users. The goal was to drive sign-ups through a warm, personalised approach rather than generic outreach.

How it worked

  • Sourced and enriched leads using Apollo
  • Built a curated prospect list in Google Sheets with personalisation fields
  • Wrote a cold email that introduced the product benefits naturally
  • Automated delivery through Zapier, pulling from the Google Sheets list
