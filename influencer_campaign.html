Simify Influencer Campaign

End-to-end mock campaign to demonstrate influencer marketing coordination and creative execution.

Project Summary

This case study simulates a full influencer campaign built for Simify, targeting young European travelers using Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts to promote eSIM services. The campaign included creator outreach, UGC collection, performance tracking, and integration with paid ads.

Tools Used

Campaign Steps

  • Discovery & Outreach: Identified 15 influencers from UK, France, and Germany. Personalized email outreach and Google Sheets tracker maintained.
  • Brief Creation: One-page brand guide and deliverable list created in Canva.
  • Content Collaboration: Reviewed and approved creator submissions.
  • Performance Tracking: Metrics logged: views, saves, shares, watch time.
  • Repurposing: Best clips were suggested for paid campaigns with custom CTAs.

Visual Samples

Key Takeaways

  • Authentic outreach = stronger response rates.
  • Story-first creators delivered the best UGC.
  • Repurposed content performed well in simulated ads.
