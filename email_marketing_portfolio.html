How I approach projects

Planning

I start by understanding the goal, the audience, and what action we want them to take. For Apex Build Co., I identified the target audience (homeowners and contractors in need of construction services), mapped out the complete customer journey from ad to consultation, and selected tools that would work within budget constraints.

Organization

I use ClickUp to break every project into clear tasks with detailed checklists and due dates. For this campaign, I created three lists: Content & Scheduling for all design and posting work, Lead Capture & Automation for forms and workflows, and Consultation Management for booking and follow-up. Each task includes specific deliverables so nothing gets overlooked.

Testing

Before any campaign goes live, I test every component multiple times. I submit forms to verify they work, check that Zapier triggers fire correctly, confirm emails arrive in inbox (not spam), test all links, and verify the experience on both mobile and desktop. I document test results with screenshots.

Documentation

I screenshot every step of the process, save all asset links, and create clear documentation as I work. This makes it easy to replicate successful campaigns, troubleshoot issues, and hand off projects to teams. It's also why I can show you exactly what I've built in this portfolio.
